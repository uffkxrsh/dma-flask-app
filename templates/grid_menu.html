FEATURES

Cut

Cut quality is categorized into Fair, Good, Very Good, Premium, and Ideal.

Clarity

Clarity is assessed on a scale from I1 (worst) to IF (best), indicating the measurement of how clear the diamond is.

Depth

Total depth percentage is calculated as z divided by the mean of x and y, or as 2 times z divided by the sum of x and y.

Color

Diamond color ranges from J (worst) to D (best).

Table

Table width refers to the width of the top of the diamond relative to its widest point and ranges from 43% to 95%.

Price

Prices range from $326 to $18,823 in US dollars.

Length(x)

Length (x): Ranges from 0 to 10.74 millimeters.

Width (y)

Width (y): Ranges from 0 to 58.9 millimeters.

Depth (z)

Depth (z): Ranges from 0 to 31.8 millimeters.
